Overview

Innova Pavimentos de Mexico is a leading company specializing in quality control laboratories and supervision for construction projects. We are dedicated to applying science and technology to transportation infrastructure, offering innovative solutions in pavement engineering, road safety, and materials analysis. With over 15 years of experience and more than 100 clients, we are committed to delivering high-quality results and ensuring the satisfaction of our clients.

Innova Today: We're heading to Milan!

This year, our focus is on building global connections. That's why our team will be attending EXPO Ferroviaria 2025 in Milan, the key meeting point for industry leaders.

From September 30th to October 2nd, we'll be at Fiera Milano Rho to connect with potential clients and partners. If you are attending and interested in our solutions, we would be delighted to schedule a meeting.

Certifications

Our commitment to quality is validated by key industry accreditations. We are proud to be recognized and accredited by the Entidad Mexicana de Acreditación (ema). This recognition ensures that our testing processes and results consistently meet the most rigorous national and international standards.
