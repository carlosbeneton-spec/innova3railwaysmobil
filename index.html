Overview

Innova Pavimentos de Mexico is a leading company specializing in quality control laboratories and supervision for construction projects. We are dedicated to applying science and technology to transportation infrastructure, offering innovative solutions in pavement engineering, road safety, and materials analysis. With over 15 years of experience and more than 100 clients, we are committed to delivering high-quality results and ensuring the satisfaction of our clients.

Our Clients

We have had the privilege of working with a wide range of clients, from government agencies to private construction firms. Our commitment to quality and precision has allowed us to build long-lasting relationships based on trust and exceptional results. We are proud to have contributed to major infrastructure projects across the country.

Innova Today

Today, Innova stands as a benchmark in the industry, continuously investing in cutting-edge technology and the training of our specialists. We are expanding our services to new areas of engineering and leading research initiatives to develop the sustainable and resilient infrastructure of the future.

Certifications

Our commitment to excellence is backed by the most rigorous industry certifications. We are certified under ISO 9001 for our quality management systems and are accredited by national entities for a wide range of material tests. These certifications guarantee that our processes and results meet the highest international standards.
