High-Speed Rail Corridor Assessment

We conducted a comprehensive track health and integrity assessment for a major high-speed rail corridor. Using ultrasonic testing and GPR, we identified critical internal defects and subgrade instabilities, allowing for targeted preventative maintenance that prevented costly service disruptions and enhanced passenger safety.

Research & Development (R&D)

Innovation is at the core of our identity. Our R&D department is dedicated to developing the next generation of infrastructure assessment tools. We are currently focused on integrating artificial intelligence with sensor data to create predictive models that can forecast rail degradation with unprecedented accuracy.
