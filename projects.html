Overview

Innova Pavimentos de Mexico is a leading company specializing in quality control laboratories and supervision for construction projects. We are dedicated to applying science and technology to transportation infrastructure, offering innovative solutions in pavement engineering, road safety, and materials analysis. With over 12 years of experience and more than 100 clients, we are committed to delivering high-quality results and ensuring the satisfaction of our clients.

Vision

Our vision is to be a leading and prestigious organization in the evaluation of the quality of road engineering projects and studies. We aim to be efficient in our technical and administrative processes, with a high level of responsiveness to our clients. We strive to be the leading construction group for industrial and commercial projects in the region by 2030.

History

Our company has a rich history of innovation and excellence in the field of pavement engineering. Since our founding, we have been committed to providing our clients with the highest quality services and solutions. We have grown from a small team of dedicated engineers to a leading firm with a reputation for excellence. We are proud of our accomplishments and look forward to continuing to serve our clients for many years to come.

Join Our Team

We are always looking for talented and motivated individuals to join our team. If you are passionate about making a difference in the world of transportation infrastructure, we encourage you to apply. We offer a dynamic and challenging work environment where you will have the opportunity to grow and develop your skills. To apply, please send your resume and cover letter to sgc@innova3.com.mx.
